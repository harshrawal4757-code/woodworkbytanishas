ABOUT US

Our Story

Est. 2021

Crafting Excellence from Jodhpur

WoodworkbyTanishas is a premium furniture manufacturing company based in Jodhpur, the historical furniture hub of India.

We have our own in-house production facility and have been serving the furniture industry since 2021. Our commitment to quality and craftsmanship has made us a trusted name in premium furniture.

We specialize in handcrafted wooden furniture including Acacia Wood, Mango Wood, Iron Range, and modern Line Range collections. Each piece is meticulously crafted by skilled artisans who carry forward generations of woodworking expertise.

Our Mission

To deliver timeless craftsmanship with premium quality materials and elegant designs, creating furniture that becomes a cherished part of homes for generations.

Quality First

Every piece undergoes rigorous quality checks to ensure durability and finish excellence.

Handcrafted

Skilled artisans handcraft each piece, bringing unique character and attention to detail.

Sustainable

We source responsibly and prioritize sustainable practices in our manufacturing process.
